# TenantShield — Product Context

## Product Purpose
TenantShield is a free web app that gives renters instant access to state-specific tenant rights and legal tools. It demystifies housing law — turning dense legal language into plain-English action steps — so tenants can protect themselves tonight, not after a lawyer appointment they can't afford.

## Register
product

## Users
Primary: US renters facing urgent housing issues — no heat, mold, eviction notices, illegal entry, deposit theft. They are stressed, often low-income, sometimes in crisis. They trust authority cues (legal language, structured formatting) and need confidence they're reading the law correctly. They are on mobile, often late at night.

Secondary: Housing advocates and legal aid workers who refer clients to the tool.

## Brand Tone
Commanding but accessible. TenantShield stands with the tenant — it's their shield. Not clinical, not corporate, not chatty. Confident, direct, urgent where needed. The voice says: "You have rights. Here they are. Use them."

## Anti-references
- iOS Settings — flat, gray, bureaucratic (current state we're moving away from)
- Generic SaaS dashboards — metric cards, gradients on numbers, hero stats
- Legal firm websites — stuffy serifs, marble textures, fake authority
- Consumer fintech — playful pastels, rounded everything, friendly-to-a-fault

## Strategic Principles
1. Trust is the product. Every visual decision must reinforce that this is accurate, serious, and on the user's side.
2. Urgency is real. Eviction notices have days. Design must communicate time-sensitivity without panic.
3. Action over information. The goal is always the next step, not the full legal picture.
4. Free and accessible. No upsell, no paywall, no dark patterns.

## Features
- My Rights: Select state + situation(s) → AI-analyzed plain-English rights + landlord obligations + tenant action steps
- Lease Analyzer: Paste a clause → AI flags illegal / suspicious / standard with plain-English translation
- Letter Generator: 3-step wizard → AI-generated formal legal letter ready to send

## States
California, Texas, New York, Florida, Illinois, Washington, Georgia, Colorado, Arizona, North Carolina
